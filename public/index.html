About Me

My Story

Hi, I am Dan Vershkov, I’m a recent graduate of Fullstack Academy coding bootcamp. Prior to my shift into software engineering I worked in a uranium geology company in Kazakhstan at several positions. So I started as an accountant, then I was transferred to the budget planning and economic department. After 6 years of working as an economist I was promoted to the position of head of geology department.

I have always been interested in web development. So, while I was working in the geology company I found part-time software development courses, so I could combine my job and studying. And I really liked it. Making small apps on my own, solving algorithm problems was really enjoyable. It started as a hobby, but then I considered why not to try to change my career into web development. Mostly I liked Javascript and Front End development, but unfortunately my software development courses didn’t pay much attention to this part of web development. That is why I decided to participate in Fullstack Academy, whose curriculum is based on JS and React development as well.

In the end I want to find a job in the IT world, most likely in front-end development, where I can grow as a professional and learn best development practices.
